Specifications

High Quality code

We strive to deliver the best quality for each script and product we offer under our brand. We produce scripts as a team, meaning that each of our members have the ability to utilise their talent. In turn, this enables us to write top notch scripts which are affordable and easy to use.

HTTP Driven Network

All CronScript products make use of the HTTP protocol in order to ensure lightweight interactions between your bots and our systems. Everything is built upon a RESTful web service which each of our products consume. This way all data is centralised and universal between all software we offer.

Security

Security becomes increasingly more important in today's day and age, and we can imagine trusting us with data regarding your bots is a sensitive topic. Our software is built with security as one of the top priorities in mind. Apart from basic crypto-methodologies like RSA, we also utilise AES and TSL/SSL. Your data is encrypted from start to end. We implemented additional precautions such as the "alias" feature in order to make your data truely anonymous.
